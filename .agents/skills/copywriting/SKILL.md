# Copywriting

Professional copywriting for digital products and marketing.

## Before Writing Any Copy

1. **Understand the product** — read all project files first
2. **Know the audience** — who are we writing for?
3. **Define the goal** — what action should the reader take?
4. **Set the tone** — professional, friendly, urgent, premium?

## Frameworks

### AIDA (Attention → Interest → Desire → Action)
Best for: landing pages, ads, email sequences

### PAS (Problem → Agitation → Solution)
Best for: sales pages, product descriptions

### BAB (Before → After → Bridge)
Best for: case studies, testimonials

## Copy Types

### Landing Page
- Hero headline (max 10 words)
- Sub-headline (1-2 sentences)
- 3-5 benefit bullets
- Social proof
- Clear CTA button

### Product Description
- What it does (1 sentence)
- Who it's for (1 sentence)
- Key benefits (3-5 bullets)
- How it works (3 steps)

### Email Sequence
- Welcome email
- Value email (teach something)
- Story email (case study)
- Offer email (CTA)
- Last chance email (urgency)

### Bot Dialogue
- Greeting message
- Menu options
- Response templates
- Error messages
- Farewell message

## Rules
1. Write in the language of the target audience
2. Use short paragraphs (1-3 sentences)
3. One idea per paragraph
4. Use power words: free, new, exclusive, proven, guaranteed
5. Always include a CTA (call to action)
6. Test headlines — write 5 variations minimum
